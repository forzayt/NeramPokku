# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. NeramPokku is designed with a strict zero-data-collection philosophy.

## 1. No Registration / Accounts
There are no signups, logins, profiles, or accounts. You enter the public message pool automatically with a random, temporary anonymous identifier generated on your browser.

## 2. No Data Collection
- **No Database**: We do not use databases, local files, or external tracking services.
- **No IP Logging**: We do not store or log your IP address.
- **No Cookies**: We do not set any tracking cookies. We only use standard `sessionStorage` in your browser to keep your temporary random username active until you close your tab.

## 3. Ephemeral Communication
- All thoughts you submit live only in the server's volatile RAM memory.
- Messages are fully erased after 2 minutes. No history or logs are maintained.

## 4. Third-Party Services
This service does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising networks.
