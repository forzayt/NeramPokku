## 1. Description of Service
NeramPokku is an ephemeral public message pool. It allows users to post short, anonymous thoughts that are broadcast to other active users and stored solely in RAM. All thoughts automatically expire and are deleted from RAM after 2 minutes.

## 2. Ephemerality & Deletion
- **No Persistence**: No thoughts, IP addresses, connection details, or user data are permanently stored or logged in any database or hard disk.
- **Auto-Expiration**: Messages are permanently purged from RAM after 2 minutes. Once deleted, they cannot be recovered by anyone.
